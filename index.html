ODA-STUDENTS-SHELF

ODA.STUDENTS-SHELF is created to make easy for our students and teachers to communicate without face to face.This website will make easy for our teachers to upload their shortnotes,so students can download from the website.The other advantage of it is that students can easily download whenever they want independently as they don't need to share it from other person.

Our Book Library Is Special Because:

Written by special teachers
All of our teachers are masters educated and they have big experience on teaching for so many years.

Supported graphically and by examples
In the website you can see that it is supported with so many examples and have great graphical expression in it.
Supported by different college books
The book is written from different sources mostly enriching student textbooks from college books which make them reliable and reliviant.
